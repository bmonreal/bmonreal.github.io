Real Time Supernova 1987A

@RealTime1987A

It's March 7, 1987. In IAUC 4334, Miriani Pasoriza and Thaisa Storlhi-Bergmann (U. Porto Alegre) report on seven nights of Balmer line blueshifts measured at the CTIO 1-m and a spectrograph called "2D-Frutti" (!). They say it's behaving like Supernova 1979C, a Type II.
