The Harvard "sky patrol" was Edward Pickering's dedicated but haphazardly-instrumented (50 years, 119500 plates) sky survey. Ralph Damon funded Donald Menzel's reboot: six identical wide-field (30°!) cameras to cover three colors (blue, yellow, red) from both hemispheres.
